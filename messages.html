Messages

The Messages section is how your supervisor and the office communicate with you through the app. There are two types of message — standard and important.

On this page
  • Reading Your Messages
  • Standard vs Important Messages
  • Message Notifications

Reading Your Messages

  1. From the main menu, tap Messages.
  2. Your message inbox will open, showing all messages sent to you, with the most recent at the top.
  3. Tap any message to open it and read the full content.
  4. Once opened, the message is marked as read.
✅

Check your messages at the start of each working day. The office and your supervisor may send updates about site conditions, schedule changes, or safety notices.

Standard vs Important Messages

Standard Message

A standard message is general information from the office or your supervisor — for example, a schedule update, a notice about a delivery, or a reminder about site procedures. Standard messages appear in your inbox list and can be read at your convenience.

  • Displayed in the normal message list
  • No acknowledgement is required
  • Shown with a standard envelope icon

Important Message

An important message is used when the office or your supervisor needs to make sure you have read and understood something — for example, a safety alert, a change to working hours, or a critical site instruction.

  • Displayed with a highlighted indicator (typically in a different colour or marked with an alert icon) so it stands out in your inbox
  • You are required to acknowledge the message by tapping a confirmation button after reading it
  • Until you acknowledge it, it may appear as a reminder each time you open the app
  • The sender can see whether you have read and acknowledged it
⚠️

Do not acknowledge an important message without reading it in full. Your acknowledgement confirms that you have understood its contents.

Message Notifications

When a new message is sent to you, a push notification will appear on your phone (if you have allowed the app to send notifications). Tapping the notification will open the message directly.

Enabling notifications

If you are not receiving notifications, check the following:

  • Go to your phone's Settings → Apps → DWCE Time Tracker → Notifications and make sure notifications are enabled
  • Ensure your phone is not in Do Not Disturb mode, which can block notifications
  • Check that you have a working internet connection — notifications require a data connection to be received
ℹ️

You cannot send messages from the worker app. Replies and queries should be directed to your supervisor by phone or in person.
