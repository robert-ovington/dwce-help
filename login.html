Login & Password

How to log in to the DWCE Time Tracker app, log out, and change your password.

On this page
  • Logging In
  • Logging Out
  • Changing Your Password
  • Trouble Logging In

Logging In

Your login credentials are set up by the office. You will receive your email address and a temporary password before your first day of use.

  1. Open the DWCE Time Tracker app on your phone.
  2. Enter your work email address in the Email field.
  3. Enter your password in the Password field.
  4. Tap Log In.
  5. The app will load your home screen and menu based on your role and permissions.

Remember Me

The app includes a Remember Me option on the login screen. When this is turned on, the app will keep you logged in between sessions so you do not need to enter your password each time you open the app.

⚠️

Only use Remember Me on your own personal phone. Do not use it on a shared or company device accessible to others.

Logging Out

  1. From the main menu, tap your profile name or the menu icon in the top corner of the screen.
  2. Scroll to the bottom of the menu and tap Log Out.
  3. You will be returned to the login screen.
✅

Any records you have already submitted are safely saved to the server. Logging out will not lose your submitted data.

Changing Your Password

You should change your password after logging in for the first time, or any time you think your password may have been seen by someone else.

  1. Open the app and log in if you are not already logged in.
  2. From the main menu, tap your profile name or the menu icon.
  3. Tap Change Password.
  4. Enter your current password.
  5. Enter your new password.
  6. Enter the new password again in the Confirm Password field.
  7. Tap Save to confirm the change.

Password tips

  • Use at least 8 characters
  • Include a mix of letters and numbers
  • Do not share your password with anyone
  • Do not use obvious information such as your name or date of birth

Trouble Logging In

Forgotten password

If you have forgotten your password, contact the office or your supervisor. A new temporary password can be reset for you by an administrator. The app does not currently have a self-service password reset link.

Incorrect email or password message

  • Check that Caps Lock is not on — passwords are case-sensitive
  • Make sure you are using your work email address, not a personal one
  • Try typing the password rather than pasting it, in case of hidden characters

App won't load after login

  • Check your internet connection — mobile data or Wi-Fi is required
  • Close the app fully and reopen it
  • If the problem continues, contact the office
ℹ️

Your account is specific to you. Do not attempt to log in using a colleague's credentials.
