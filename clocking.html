Clocking In & Out

Use the Clocking screen to record when you arrive at and leave a work site. Your location is captured at the time of clocking to confirm site attendance.

Clocking In

Clock in as soon as you arrive on site at the start of your working day or shift.

  1. From the main menu, tap Clock In / Out.
  2. The screen will display your current status — if you are not yet clocked in it will show Not Clocked In.
  3. The app will read your current GPS location. Ensure you are on or near the work site before proceeding.
  4. Tap the Clock In button.
  5. A confirmation message will appear showing the time your clock-in was recorded.
✅

Clock in before you start any work, not at the end of the day. The time recorded is the exact moment you tap the button.

Clocking Out

Clock out when you are about to leave the site at the end of your working day or shift.

  1. From the main menu, tap Clock In / Out.
  2. The screen will show your current status — if you are clocked in it will show your clock-in time and a Clock Out button.
  3. Make sure you are still on site before tapping.
  4. Tap Clock Out.
  5. A confirmation message will appear showing your clock-out time. Your total hours for that attendance record will be displayed.
⚠️

Do not clock out and then continue working. Clock out only when you are genuinely finishing for the day.

Clocking In at the Office

If you are based at the office rather than on a construction site, there is a separate Office Clock In / Out screen. The process is identical to site clocking — tap Clock In when you arrive and Clock Out when you leave.

ℹ️

The Office clock-in screen may only appear in your menu if your role is set up for office attendance. If you cannot see it and think you should, contact the office.

Location Permission Required

The Clocking screen requires your phone's GPS to be active. If the app cannot read your location, the Clock In / Out button will be disabled or an error will appear.

Checklist if location is not working

  • Make sure your phone's Location / GPS is switched on in your phone's settings
  • Check that the DWCE Time Tracker app has been granted location permission — go to Settings → Apps → DWCE Time Tracker → Permissions → Location → Allow While Using App
  • Stand in an open area away from buildings if possible — GPS accuracy improves in open spaces
  • Wait a few seconds and try again — GPS can take a moment to get a fix

Missed a Clocking

If you forgot to clock in or out, you cannot add a missed clocking yourself through the app. Report the missed clocking to your supervisor who can amend your attendance record from the admin side of the system.

ℹ️

Your timesheet (added separately) is not the same as your clocking record. You can still submit a timesheet for a day even if the clocking record needs to be corrected.
